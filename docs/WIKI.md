## What Neon Fabs Is

Neon Fabs is an asynchronous economy game about running city-installed fabs, collecting printed components, building reputation, trading through local markets, and sending vehicles across routes.

Your long-term goal is to build reputation. Credits, items, fabs, routes, and completed patterns are all tools for becoming known.

## First Session

1. Choose a home city.
2. Open your first Print Bay output.
3. Store the printed components in city inventory.
4. Complete your first pattern in your home city.
5. Use markets, fabs, and routes to build from there.

Contracts guide the early steps. Later, the game should leave more room for exploration and strategy.

## Core Systems

### Fabs

Fabs are city-installed printers. They create sealed Print Bay output over real time while battery is active.

### Print Bay

The Print Bay holds sealed output until you open it in the city where the fab is installed. Opening output reveals what was printed and recharges battery.

### Inventory

Inventory is local to each city. Items bought, printed, or shipped to a city stay in that city until moved.

### Patterns

Patterns are collections made from printed components in your home city. Completed patterns increase battery capacity and reputation.

The word "meld" is still provisional in prototype files.

### Market

Markets are city-local. Sellers post listings, buyers post bids, and prices come from those orders.

### Dispatch

Dispatch is route work. Merchants move cargo. Routejacks send raid convoys against designed NPC targets. Route encounters are PvE in the current prototype.
