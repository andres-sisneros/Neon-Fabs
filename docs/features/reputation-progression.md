# Reputation Progression

## Goal

Give every player a long-term reason to keep printing, trading, collecting, and routing goods: build a public reputation in the world.

## First Slice

- Show total Rep in the header and Profile.
- Give the player a reputation title based on total Rep.
- Award Collection Rep when a meld pattern is completed.
- Show a small local reputation board with placeholder NPC operators.
- Keep "meld" as a provisional system name until the canon naming pass.

## Current Reward Tuning

- Common pattern: 10 Rep
- Uncommon pattern: 25 Rep
- Rare pattern: 60 Rep
- Epic pattern: 160 Rep
- Legendary pattern: 400 Rep

## Later

- Market Rep for fulfilled buy orders, profitable sales, and supplying scarce goods.
- Route Rep for deliveries, route clears, and routejack rewards.
- Fab Rep for rare output records, equipment progression, and machine mastery.
- Seasonal and city-specific boards so newer players have attainable goals.
- Public profile showcases for favorite completed patterns, rare output, and role accomplishments.
