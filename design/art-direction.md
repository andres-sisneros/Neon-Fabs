# Art Direction

## Current Direction

Readable pixel-art inspired UI moments with clean browser-game surfaces. City scenes should feel animated and alive, like small Steam-sale-style loops, while UI remains tight enough for phone check-ins.

## Asset Buckets

- `assets/cities/`: city atmosphere scenes and map markers.
- `assets/items/`: item icons, meld card art, gear icons.
- `assets/fabs/`: printer loops and fab-type silhouettes.
- `assets/routes/`: road, sea, convoy, and encounter art.
- `assets/ui/`: borders, symbols, rarity treatments, buttons.

## Near-Term Art Needs

1. Starter Fab printer loop variants for 1, 2, and 3 active fabs.
2. City vibe panels for Chrome Pier, Lowline, Orchid Sprawl, Vanta Arcology, and Helix Quay.
3. Meld card art direction: the meld page already behaves like collectible set cards, so each meld could eventually get a small card image.

## Constraints

- Avoid art that requires tiny text to understand.
- Keep assets legible on an iPhone 13 mini.
- Use motion to signal state: printing, sealed output, route travel, encounter, completed set.
