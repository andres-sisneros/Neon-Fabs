# Naming Workbench

Use this as a scratchpad before names are promoted to `content/creative-overrides.js`.

## Naming Rules

- Common items should sound street-level, tactile, and useful.
- Higher-rarity names can imply institutions, rituals, sealed history, or strange civic tech.
- Avoid direct borrowed nouns from inspiration sources.
- Favor names that suggest what the thing feels like, not only what it does.
